Privacy Policy - MemoryMate

Last updated: February 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MemoryMate collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (hashed)
  • Profile Data: Avatar selection, color preferences, friend list
  • Game Data: Scores, game statistics, achievements, play history
  • Device Information: Device type, OS, app version (for analytics)
  • Language & Settings: Preferred language, sound settings (stored locally)
  • Chat Messages: Messages sent during multiplayer games (stored for game duration)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate and authorize users
  • Display leaderboards and rankings
  • Enable multiplayer matchmaking and friend features
  • Deliver personalized game content and achievements
  • Improve game experience and identify bugs
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Send optional notifications about updates

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in MongoDB with encryption at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We use SSL/TLS for all data transmission. We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing subscriptions and purchases
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Analytics Providers: For aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

4. Advertising & Analytics

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. This service may collect device information, ad identifiers, and usage patterns for personalized ads. See Google's privacy policy for details.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store credit card information; Google Play handles all payment processing securely.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

MemoryMate is suitable for all ages. Users under 13 (or the age of digital consent in their country):

  • Should have parental consent before creating an account
  • Cannot participate in chat or friend systems without additional verification
  • Are not targeted with personalized advertisements

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data (30-day grace period)
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads or notifications

To exercise these rights, email: hsezginefe@gmail.com

8. Data Deletion Process

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the app and go to Menu → Profile → Logout
  2. Or email us at hsezginefe@gmail.com with your username
  3. Your account will be marked for deletion (30-day grace period)
  4. All data (profile, scores, friends, messages) will be permanently deleted

9. Cookies & Local Storage

The web version uses:

  • Session Cookies: For authentication (7 days)
  • Local Storage: For game preferences and language selection
  • Third-Party Cookies: AdMob (for ads)

10. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Permanently deleted after 30-day grace period
  • Chat Messages: Deleted 30 days after game session
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months

11. Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

  • Google Play Services: App distribution, billing, push notifications
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement delivery

Each service has its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for their practices.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Your continued use of MemoryMate after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: hsezginefe@gmail.com
  • Support: hsezginefe@gmail.com

Response time: We respond to all requests within 15 business days.
